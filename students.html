PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS

I encourage you to read a few of my recent papers to get a sense of the work we do. If you are already at UMD, feel free to reach out and let me know what you are looking for — advisor, committee member, or research experience (note to undergraduates/MS students: RAships are rarely available for students I haven't worked with).

If you are looking to apply to the PhD program, please write me with a brief note on what interests you. Include [Prospective Student: Your Name] in the subject line and attach your resume or CV. Due to the volume of emails I receive, I may not be able to reply to all emails and meeting requests — I appreciate your understanding.
